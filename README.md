# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video — Content Performance Dashboard

> **An end-to-end data analysis project** exploring content trends, genre performance, regional distribution, and IMDb ratings across Amazon Prime Video's library of 8,861+ titles.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Amazon Prime Video content catalog to uncover strategic insights for content acquisition, production decisions, and regional distribution. The analysis is powered by a Tableau dashboard and supported by exploratory data analysis (EDA) in Python.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Content | 8,861 titles |
| Movies | 7,852 |
| TV Shows | 1,009 |
| Top Producing Countries | US, Canada, India |

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify the most popular genres by volume vs. by audience rating
- Analyze regional content distribution and gaps
- Track how the content library has evolved over time
- Surface top-rated titles and actors
- Provide strategic recommendations for content planning

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. 📊 Genre Strategy
- **Drama, Comedy, and Thriller** dominate by volume
- **Documentaries, Reality Shows, and History** score higher in audience ratings
- → Opportunity: Invest in high-rated but underrepresented genres

### 2. 🌍 Regional Distribution
- **US, Canada, and India** are the most active content-producing countries
- Underserved regions represent growth opportunities for localization
- → Strategy: Partner with local creators for regional content

### 3. 📺 TV Shows vs. Movies
- TV shows are significantly underrepresented vs. movies
- Multi-season series drive stronger long-term engagement
- → Recommendation: Increase TV show acquisitions and originals

### 4. 📅 Content Timeline
- Content is added in **specific bursts** with noticeable gaps
- → Fill gaps with short-form specials and seasonal content

### 5. 📈 Trend Forecasting
- Documentary, History, and Reality genres show **upward rating trends**
- These signal a shift in viewer preferences worth acting on early
